Pizza

Description

Quick to prepare snack.

Ingredients

  • Pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • garlic
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread pesto on pizza crust. Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, garlic, and cheese
  3. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned.
